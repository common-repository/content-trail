Displays Related Posts in Non English languages. Also shows Tag clouds, Popular content, Personalised content for every user! Optimises page views

== Description ==

Our powerful plugin gathers all of the website’s posts, understands the semantics/context of every post   and the interests of every user through NLP/AI to suggest relevant posts. This allows you to increase reader’s engagement by helping them find content that they would prefer.

What our plugin shows:

- Related posts based on the similarity between posts/content. Our Machine learning algorithm identifies and categorises posts as related to politics, sports, technology, etc,  Personalities, Countries, Organisations, etc mentioned in posts are identified and similar posts are suggested based on this. 
- Personalised posts for every user. His interest topics are captured by our plugin to suggest highly relevant posts for the user.
- Trending posts /Popular posts
- Tag clouds for posts. The most important tags in a post are auto-identified by our algorithm to produce tag clouds.

Our recommendation algorithm works with Non English content as well. Our plugin understands content in any language to recommend related content in the same language or across languages.

Benefits you will see:

- More clicks on articles
- More time spent on site
- Retain visitors/users
- Reduce bounce rates
- Cross suggestions of content across multiple languages

== Installation ==
From Wordpress dashboard:
Visit `Plugins > Add New`
Search for ‘User Related Posts`
Activate the plugin

From Wordpress.org
- Download the plugin
- Extract the zipped folder
- Paste in wp-content/plugins directory of Wordpress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Is User Related Posts free? =
Yes, all features of Content Trail like recommendations, personalisation,trending and multi-language recommendations for Wordpress posts are currently free up till a certain number of requests. Subsequent versions might charge a fee based on the usage metrics.

= How do I enable multi language recommendations? =
User Related Posts plugin will pool all the content on your website and will automatically start recommending content across languages. Nothing extra needs to be done to enable multi-language recommendations.

= How do I activate the plugin? =
- Go to plugin menu in Wordpress admin
- Activate the User Related Posts plugin
- You will find a separate menu option \"User Related Posts Plugin\"
- Click on that and provide email id where a key will be send. 
- Copy that key from provided email and paste it to \”User Related Posts Plugin\" page.
- Click on verify, once verified you are ready to go, else you will get message invalid key.

= How do I add the plugin to my page? =
- Goto Appearance->widget. There you will find three widgets. \”User Related Posts Recommendation\", \”User Related Posts Personalisation\", \”User Related Posts Trending\".
- Place each widget on your required page, like sidebars.
- Wait for 10 minutes while we process your data
- Refresh the page where you installed the widget and you will get to see the widget working

= How can I configure my widget? =

Click on User Related Posts plugin, then click on User Related Posts widget next to Configure your widget. You'll be allowed to configure the following aspects:
- The Widget can show images and/or text
- How the widget layout should appear
- The look of the widget
